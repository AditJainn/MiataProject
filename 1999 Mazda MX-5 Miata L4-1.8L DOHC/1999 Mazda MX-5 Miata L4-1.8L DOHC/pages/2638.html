Fuel Leakage Inspection

WARNING:Fuel line spills and leaks are dangerous. Fuel can ignite and cause serious injuries or death and damage. Always carry out the following procedure with the engine stopped.

CAUTION: Connecting the wrong data link connector terminals may possibly cause a malfunction. Carefully connect the specified terminals only.




1. Connect data link connector terminals F/P and GND by using a jumper wire.
2. Turn the ignition switch to ON to operate the fuel pump.
3. Pressurize the system this way for at least 5 minutes to be sure of no leakage.
4. If there is fuel leakage, inspect for damaged fuel hoses, hose clamps, and fuel pipe sealing surface and replace as necessary.
5. After repair, assemble the system and repeat steps 1 to 3.
