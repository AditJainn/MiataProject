Seat Belt Buckle Switch: Testing and Inspection



1. Remove the driver's side quarter trim.
2. Disconnect the seat belt switch connector.
3. Slide the driver's side seat backward as far as possible.






4. Inspect for continuity between the seat belt switch terminals by using an ohmmeter.
5. If not as specified, replace the seat belt.
