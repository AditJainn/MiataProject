Oil Pump: Specifications



OIL PUMP SPECIFICATIONS

Inner Rotor Clearance
Standard Tooth Tip 0.02-0.18 mm (0.0008-0.0070 in)
Maximum Tooth Tip 0.20 mm (0.0079 in)
Outer Roter Clearance
Standard 0.090-0.176 mm (0.0036-0.0069 in)

Maximum 0.14mm (0.0055)
