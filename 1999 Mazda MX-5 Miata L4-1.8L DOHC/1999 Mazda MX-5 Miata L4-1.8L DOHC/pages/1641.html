Brake Pedal Assy

TIGHTENING SPECIFICATIONS:
Push Rod Locknut 24 - 34 Nm (17 - 25 ft. lbs.)
Pivot Bolt/Nut 20 - 34 Nm (14 - 25 ft. lbs.)

GENERAL SPECIFICATIONS:
Pedal Height (Reference Value) [1] 171 - 181 mm (6.73 - 7.13 inch)
Free Play 4.0 - 8.4 mm (0.16 - 0.33 inch)
Pedal To Floor Clearance [2] 95 mm (3.74 inch) minimum

[1]: With Carpet

[2]: Pedal Depressed With A Force Of 589 N (132 lbs.), Without Carpet.
