Clockspring Assembly / Spiral Cable: Testing and Inspection

1. Remove the clock spring. (Refer to CLOCK SPRING REMOVAL/INSTALLATION.)






2. Inspect for continuity between the clock spring terminals by using an ohmmeter.

NOTE: When terminals 3A and 3B are disconnected from the vehicle's main harness, they are shorted to prevent unexpected air bag deployment.

3. If not as specified, replace the clock spring.
