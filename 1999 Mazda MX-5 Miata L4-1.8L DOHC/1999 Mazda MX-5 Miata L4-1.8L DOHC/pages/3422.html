Front

DISC PAD THICKNESS INSPECTION
1. On level ground, jack up the front of the vehicle and support it with safety stands.
2. Remove the wheels.
3. Look through the caliper inspection hole and verify the remaining thickness of the pad.

Thickness: 1.0 mm (0.04 inch) min.
