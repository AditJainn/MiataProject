Reverse Idler Gear Assembly Note

Reverse Idler Gear Assembly Note





1. Install the reverse idler gear, and washer, and bearing cover.
2. Tighten the bearing cover installation bolts.

Tightening torque
A: 20 - 26 N-m (2.1 - 2.6 kgf-m, 15 - 19 ft-lbf)
B: 36 - 54 N-m (3.7 - 5.5 kgf-m, 27 - 39 ft-lbf)
