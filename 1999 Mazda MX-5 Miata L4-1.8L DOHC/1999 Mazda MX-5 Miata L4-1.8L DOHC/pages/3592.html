Tie Rod: Specifications

TIGHTENING SPECIFICATIONS:
Locknut 35 - 50 Nm (26 - 36 ft. lbs.)
To Steering Knuckle Nut 43 - 56 Nm (32 - 41 ft. lbs.)
With Manual Gear:
To Steering Rack 79 - 98 Nm (58 - 72 ft. lbs.)
With Power Gear:
To Steering Rack 59 - 78 Nm (44 - 57 ft. lbs.)


GENERAL SPECIFICATIONS:
Rotation Torque 0.3 - 2.9 Nm (2.6 - 26 inch lbs.)
Pull Scale Reading 3 - 29 N (0.7 - 6.6 lbs.)
Swinging Torque 0.5 - 3.1 Nm (4.4 - 27.7 inch lbs.)
Pull Scale Reading 3.0 - 19.6 N (0.7 - 4.4 lbs.)
