Condenser Fan Motor: Testing and Inspection

1. Disconnect the condenser tan connector.




2. Connect battery positive voltage to terminal A and ground to terminal B of the condenser fan, and verify that air blows towards the engine.
3. It the condenser tan does not operate, replace the condenser tan.
