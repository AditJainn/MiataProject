Overdrive Switch: Service and Repair

O/D OFF SWITCH REMOVAL/INSTALLATION
1. Disconnect the negative battery cable.
2. Remove the rear console.
3. Disconnect the connector and remove the O/D OFF switch terminals.
4. Remove the selector lever knob component.
5. Install a selector lever knob component.
Tightening torque 2.0 - 2.9 Nm (20 - 30 kgf cm, 18 - 26 inch lbs.)
6. Install the O/D OFF switch terminals and connect the connector.
7. Install the rear console.
8. Connect the negative battery cable.
