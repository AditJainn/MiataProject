Control Arm

LOWER ARM:

Front:
Pivot Bolts/Nuts 94 - 112 Nm (69 - 83 ft. lbs.)

Rear:
To Crossmember Pivot Bolts/Nuts 73 - 95 Nm (54 - 70 ft. lbs.)
To Knuckle Pivot Bolt/Nut 63 - 74 Nm (63 - 73 ft. lbs.)

UPPER ARM:

Front:
Pivot Bolt/Nut 118 - 137 Nm (87 - 101 ft. lbs.)

Rear:
Pivot Bolts/Nuts 54 - 76 Nm (40 - 56 ft. lbs.)
