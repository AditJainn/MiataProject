Ignition On Test

1. Perform steps 1 through 8 from the DTCs Reading Procedure.




2. Turn ignition switch on. Move the cursor to SIMULATION TEST. Press the trigger key to enter this selection.




3. Move the cursor to IGNITION ON TEST. Press the trigger key to enter this selection.




4. The screen will display a list of PIDs. Select the appropriate PID for testing, then press trigger.




5. Press START.
6. Press trigger key.




7. The simulation is performed for 3 seconds, and a "SIMULATION ACTIVATED PLEASE WAIT" message is displayed during those 3 seconds.




8. To perform the simulation again, press the trigger key. To exit the ignition on test, press the cancel key.
