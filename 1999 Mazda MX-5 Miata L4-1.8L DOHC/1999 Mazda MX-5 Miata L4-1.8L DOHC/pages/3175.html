Shift-Lock Actuator Removal/Installation

SHIFT-LOCK ACTUATOR REMOVAL/INSTALLATION
1. Disconnect the negative battery cable.
2. Remove the rear console.
3. Remove the indicator screws and lift up the indicator panel.
4. Disconnect the shift-lock actuator connector.
5. Disconnect the P position switch connector.
6. Remove the shift-lock actuator.
7. Install a shift-lock actuator.
Tightening torque 0.7 - 1.2 Nm (7 - 13 kgf-cm, 6.1 - 11.2 inch lbs.)
8. Connect the P position switch connector.
9. Connect the shift-lock actuator connector.





10. Install and adjust the indicator panel.
Tightening torque 2.0 - 2.9 Nm (20 - 30 kgf-cm, 18 - 26 inch lbs.)
11. Install the rear console.
12. Verify correct operation of the shift-lock system.
13. Connect the negative battery cable.
