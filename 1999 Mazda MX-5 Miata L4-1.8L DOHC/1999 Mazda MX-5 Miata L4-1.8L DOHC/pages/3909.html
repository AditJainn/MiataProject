Air Bag Modules Deployment Authorization Procedures

1. After installing a new SAS control module, verify that the air bag system warning light flashes continuously approximately 6 seconds after the ignition switch is turned to ON.
2. If a diagnostic trouble code is indicated, perform the appropriate AIR BAG SYSTEM ON-BOARD DIAGNOSIS. (Refer to AIR BAG SYSTEM ON-BOARD DIAGNOSIS.)
3. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK then back to ON while the air bag system warning light is flashing continuously. If the procedures have been performed correctly, the air bag system warning light illuminates, then goes oft after approximately 6 seconds.
4. If it does not go off, perform the deployment authorization procedure again.
