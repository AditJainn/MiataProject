Year 2000 - Computer Systems Compliance

Bulletin No 018/99
Issued 05/28/99
Revised

Section
01

Applicable Model/s All

Subject
YEAR 2000 COMPLIANCE

APPLICABLE MODEL(S)/VINS
All models.

DESCRIPTION

Because all Control Units, including the Powertrain Control Modules used in Mazda vehicles do not use the day, month, or year to operate, Mazda vehicles are completely immune to the effects of the year 2000 concern.
