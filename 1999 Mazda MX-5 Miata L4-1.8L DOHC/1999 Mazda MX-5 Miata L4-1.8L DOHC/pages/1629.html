Ball Joint, Lower

TIGHTENING SPECIFICATIONS:
Castle Nut 55 - 77 Nm (42 - 57 ft. lbs.)
Through Bolt/Nut 94 - 116 Nm (69 - 86 ft. lbs.)
Mounting Bolt 73 - 93 Nm (54 - 68 ft. lbs.)

GENERAL SPECIFICATIONS:
Rotation Torque 0.35 - 1.8 Nm (3.1 - 16.9 inch lbs.)
Pull Scale Reading 3.5 - 18.2 Nm (0.77 - 4.29 lbs.)
