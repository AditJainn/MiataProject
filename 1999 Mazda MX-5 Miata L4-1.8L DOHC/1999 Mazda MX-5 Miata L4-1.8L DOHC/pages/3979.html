Rear Antenna Feeder

REAR ANTENNA FEEDER REMOVAL/INSTALLATION
1. Disconnect the negative battery cable.
2. Remove the trunk side trim (LH).
3. Disconnect the connection between the rear antenna feeder and the power antenna. (With manual antenna: manual antenna)
4. Remove the rear package trim.
5. Turn over the floor covering from the right side.
6. Disconnect the connection between the front antenna feeder and the rear antenna feeder.




7. Remove in the order indicated in the table.
8. Install in the reverse order of removal.
