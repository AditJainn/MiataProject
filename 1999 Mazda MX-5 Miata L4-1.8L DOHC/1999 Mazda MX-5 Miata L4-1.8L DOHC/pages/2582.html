Fuel Pump Relay Inspection

NOTE: The lock of the fuel pump relay will be easily broken when removing the relay from the bracket. Do not remove the relay from the bracket except when replacement is required. Perform "FUEL PUMP RELAY INSPECTION" before removing the relay.
