30000 MI or 48000 KM

Lubricate
    Doors, Hood and Trunk
      Lubricate all locks and hinges.
Inspect
    Steering
      Inspect steering operation and linkages.
    Idle Speed
      According to state and federal regulations, failure to perform maintenance on this item will not void your emissions warranties. However, Mazda recommends that all maintenance services be performed at the recommended time or mileage period to ensure long-term reliability.
    Heating and Air Conditioning
      Inspect refrigerant and compressor (if equipped).
    Heat Shield, Exhaust
    Fuel Supply Line
      According to State and Federal regulations, failure to perform maintenance on this item will not void your emissions warranties. However, Mazda recommends that all maintenance services be performed at the recommended time or mileage period to ensure long-term reliability.
    Drive Belt
      Inspect tension of all drive belts.
    Disc Brake System
    Cooling System
    Constant Velocity Joint Boot
      Inspect driveshaft dust boots every 30,000 miles or 24 months whichever comes first.
    Brake Hose/Line
    Body and Frame
      Inspect bolts and nuts on chassis and body.
    Ball Joint
      Inspect suspension ball joints.
Replace
    Spark Plug
    Oil Filter, Engine
    Engine Oil
    Air Filter Element
Rotate
    Tires
