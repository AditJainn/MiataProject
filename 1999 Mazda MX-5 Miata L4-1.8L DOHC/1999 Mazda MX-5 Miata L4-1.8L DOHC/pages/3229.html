Shifter A/T: Testing and Inspection

SELECTOR LEVER INSPECTION





1. Verify that the selector lever can be shifted only as shown in the figure.
2. Make sure there is a click at each range when shifted from P position to 1 range.
3. Verify that the positions of the selector lever and the indicator are aligned.
4. Verify that the selector lever moves smoothly.
5. If not correct, adjust or repair the selector lever.
