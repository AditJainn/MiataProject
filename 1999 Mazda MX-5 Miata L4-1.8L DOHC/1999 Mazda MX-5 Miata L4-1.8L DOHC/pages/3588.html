Steering Wheel: Specifications

TIGHTENING SPECIFICATIONS:
Retaining Nut 40 - 49 Nm (29 - 36 ft. lbs.)

GENERAL SPECIFICATIONS:

Manual Gear:
Play 0 - 30 mm (0 - 1.18 inch)
Effort [1] 5 - 29 N (1.1 - 6.6 lbs.)

Power Gear:
Play 0 - 30 mm (0 - 1.18 inch)
Effort [1] 7.8 N (6.9 lbs.)

[1]: During One Turn Of Steering Wheel.
