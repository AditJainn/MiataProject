Headlamp Switch: Testing and Inspection

COMBINATION SWITCH INSPECTION

Headlight Switch
1. Remove the combination switch.







2. Inspect for continuity between the combination switch terminals by using an ohmmeter.
3. If not as specified, replace the light unit or light lever.

Turn Switch
1. Remove the combination switch.







2. Inspect for continuity between the combination switch terminals by using an ohmmeter.
3. If not as specified, replace the light unit or light lever.
