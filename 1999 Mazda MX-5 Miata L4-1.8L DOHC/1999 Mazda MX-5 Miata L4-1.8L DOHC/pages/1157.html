Diagnostic Aids

- Thoroughly read and understand the basic flow of troubleshooting to properly perform the procedures.
- For the steps that have an asterisk(*), inspect the connector/terminal connection for continuity and damage. If the connection is poor, reconnect it, or repair or replace the appropriate parts as necessary.
- The areas for inspection (steps) are given according to various circuit malfunctions. Use the chart to verify the symptoms of the trouble in order to diagnose the appropriate area.
