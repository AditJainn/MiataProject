Fuel Pressure Pulsation Damper: Testing and Inspection

1. Visually inspect pulsation damper No.1 and No.2 for damage and cracks. Also inspect that there is no extreme rust which will cause fuel leakage.
2. If either is observed, replace the faulty pulsation damper.
