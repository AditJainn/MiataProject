Ignition Switch: Testing and Inspection

IGNITION SWITCH INSPECTION
1. Remove the column cover.
2. Disconnect the ignition switch connector.




3. Inspect for continuity between the ignition switch terminals by using an ohmmeter.




4. If not as specified, replace the ignition switch.
